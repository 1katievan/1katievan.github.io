Smash Bros Poster Designs

Posters featuring popular super smash bros characters for a gaming event.

About

Being a fan of Nintendo's Smash Bros games (being a prolific smash bros gamer myself), I created some posters of some if the most popular characters from Smash Bros melee.

Captain Falcon | Knee of Justice

Captain Falcon is an iconic character who is best known for his 'forward air' move, in which he knees his opponents mid-air to send them flying off the stage. This is a good finisher to combos and it is an exciting event anytime someone is able to pull off a knee of justice.

See the Captain Falcon speed paint video below.

The Peachy Truth

For the deadly Peach painting, I decided to go between a merge of cartoon and realistic. Her Peach is pictured with a somewhat blood stained dress. This is because in the game she has a deadly attack that people spam in which her dress does ridiculous amounts of damage to the opponent. Furthermore, the largest competitive tournament was won two years in a row by a Peach player.

See the Princess Peach speed paint video below.

Fox vs. Falco | Yin-Yang

Fox and Falco are the two best characters in melee (in terms of fighting ability), and in competitive play are the two most common characters used. Because of this, I decided to create the two characters fighting with gameCube controllers in a ying yang symbol. (Melee is played using GameCube controllers competitively.)
