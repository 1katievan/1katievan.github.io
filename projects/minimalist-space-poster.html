Minimalist Space Poster

A minimalistic styled poster with retro textures created to advertise my client's gaming club.

About

My client wanted an epic poster to recruit pilots for his IDA (https://www.yourgalaxy.org/) group for Elite:Dangerous (video game).

I created the poster to reflect the minimalist style of the reference posters, as well as the beautiful in-game world of Elite:Dangerous and it's amazing spaceship designs.

My client is now has an epic poster, as well as the original artwork, to be showcased on on Reddit, Imgur, forums and so on to find new recruits.

Original freelance description at https://www.upwork.com/jobs/~0139b872703384c365. Permission was granted to share this as a portfolio piece.
