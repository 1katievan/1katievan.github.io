Sci-fi Alien Landscapes | Concept Art

Fascinating alien landscapes on a planet with harsh living conditions and a strange solar system.

About

This project is part of the Planet K8T0 series, a string of assignments in which I and two other students got the opportunity to be mentored by an exceptional illustrator local to my area. The point of the assignments was to exercise not only illustrative abilities but design thinking in regards to a cohesive narrative and world.

This series of landscape concepts explore the possible biomes that could exist on the planet, and include a scorching desert that is constantly touched by sun most of the year, a cooler red alloy desert with phosphorus rivers, the dark icelands which almost never see sun, and the watery jungle caverns, which exist in the oceanic areas which exist in the planets massive craters.

Tools

These digital paintings were created in Photoshop.

Skills

Illustration, Digital Painting, Concept Art, Photoshop, Pencil and paper sketching.
