About

This short video was a rushed project to make a school presentation even cooler. It worked!

I created this video as part of the HackerHood series, a school project in which I and three other teammates created a videogame called HackerHood.

The video is to describe the initial concept ideas our group had for the game for our school presentation, and give our viewers something a little extra so they would be excited for our project. The illustrations are all in a similar grunge and painterly style to better express the mood of the game. The video was very rushed, and only meant to be the extra icing on a presentation, so the storyboard, narration, illustrations, music, and video animation were all created within 24 hours.
