Desert Oasis | Sci-fi Concept Illustration

A personal art study combining the concepts of 'desert oasis' and 'ww2 submarine'.

About

This strange mysterious artwork features the peculiar phenomenon of a WWII submarine being found overtop a desert oasis. It was inspired by combining two concept which can seem to be oxymorons; a desert and a submarine, which can lead to interesting results. It this art work, I used areas of shade and brightness to help create a sense of vast space and depth, and a trail of people going towards the tree for some added mystery and narrative.

Tools

This digital concept art was created in Photoshop using both both photobashing and digital painting techniques.
