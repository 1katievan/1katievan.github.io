Female Armor Suit | Sci-fi Concept Art

A sci-fi concept illustration for an armor suit designed for exploration of an alien planet.

About

This combined armor and character concept explores a possibility in space armor for humans investigating the surface of alien planets. The armor makes use of rose gold to convey elegance while still keeping sharp and blunt use of shape similar to the architecture of the human colonies in this series. This was created for a series of assignments in which I and two other students got the opportunity to be mentored by an exceptional illustrator local to my area.

Most of the design process took place on paper. Color choice and painting individual details were done digitally. I used Photoshop for my digital illustration needs.

The design for the alien species she encounters is inspired by shrimp shape and anatomy.

Tools

This digital painting was created in Photoshop.
