Promo Art for Indie Game Studio | Digital Painting

Promo art created by combining two images with digital painting techniques.

About

This promotional artwork was created to be a feature image for the homepage of another of my own websites, KatarStudios.Github.io. This is an indie game website to list games I have created and published, and to blog about indie game development.

The image was created with Photoshop by combining a screenshot of the most recently upcoming game and a photo of my desk, and digitally painting over both with a painterly style to have a cohesive and interesting style. I added a dark ambience to match the horror-esque game genre, and high contrast colors to help pop and coordinate with the rest of the website.
