About

A book cover illustration featuring custom typography and a grand view of the character standing before a ruined sunset city.

Because I want to illustrate books and posters, I decided to create a demonstration of my artwork for a character I developed in a different post named Key.

Through this illustration, my main goal was to capture the essence of the city. The custom typographic text that combines design elements of keys with a clay-like texture, which are both relevant to the storyline I had developed.

Tools

This digital concept art was created in Photoshop using digital painting techniques.
