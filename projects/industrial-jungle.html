Industrial Jungle | Concept Illustration Study

A personal art study where I turned a photo of an XBox and house plant into a sci-fi universe.

About

As a personal creative exercise, I took a strange photo of my Rec. Room and used it as my color palette and inspiration for an environmental concept piece. The photo is very strange and can be hard to make out, but pictured below is actually an X-Box (with the case removed), with a potted plant in the background.

The colors and shapes in the photo made me think of a military jungle. So I started blocking in the digital paint to turn it into one. Below are the progress pics, first with the colors and crude line art blocked in, then with the details minus the characters.

Tools

This digital concept art was created in Photoshop using both both photobashing and digital painting techniques.
