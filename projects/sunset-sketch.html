Sunset Sketch | Digital Painting

A digital painting that resulted from an experimental digital brush study.

About

This artwork was the result of a personal experiment with focus on trying out digital brushes, using colors opposite from each other on the color wheel, and composition. I discoverd that digital brushes can acheive textures that create a 'painterly' look to them, while achieving bright and vibrant colors sometimes not possible with real paint.

Tools

This digital painting was created in Photoshop.
